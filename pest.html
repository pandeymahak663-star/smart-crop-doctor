🐛 Pest Control Guide

Aphids

Damage: Suck plant sap and weaken crops.

Organic: Neem oil spray.

Chemical: Imidacloprid spray.

Prevention: Remove infected plants.

Whiteflies

Damage: Spread plant viruses.

Organic: Yellow sticky traps.

Chemical: Pyrethrin spray.

Prevention: Maintain crop spacing.

Cutworms

Damage: Cut seedlings at soil level.

Organic: Bacillus thuringiensis.

Chemical: Chlorpyrifos.

Prevention: Clean soil.

Spider Mites

Damage: Leaves turn yellow.

Organic: Neem oil.

Chemical: Abamectin.

Prevention: Maintain humidity.

Thrips

Damage: Silver streaks on leaves.

Organic: Blue sticky traps.

Chemical: Spinosad spray.

Prevention: Remove infected plants.

Armyworms

Damage: Eat leaves rapidly.

Organic: Bacillus thuringiensis.

Chemical: Chlorantraniliprole.

Prevention: Monitor crops.

Leaf Miners

Damage: Tunnels in leaves.

Organic: Neem oil.

Chemical: Spinosad.

Prevention: Remove infected leaves.

Mealybugs

Damage: White cotton insects sucking sap.

Organic: Soap water spray.

Chemical: Imidacloprid.

Prevention: Clean plants.

Fruit Borers

Damage: Bore holes in fruits.

Organic: Pheromone traps.

Chemical: Emamectin Benzoate.

Prevention: Remove infected fruits.

Stem Borers

Damage: Bore stems.

Organic: Neem spray.

Chemical: Carbofuran.

Prevention: Field sanitation.

Grasshoppers

Damage: Eat leaves.

Organic: Garlic spray.

Chemical: Malathion.

Prevention: Remove weeds.

Locusts

Damage: Destroy crops in swarms.

Organic: Neem spray.

Chemical: Organophosphate insecticide.

Prevention: Early monitoring.

Termites

Damage: Attack plant roots.

Organic: Neem cake.

Chemical: Chlorpyrifos.

Prevention: Maintain soil moisture.

Leafhoppers

Damage: Leaf curling.

Organic: Neem spray.

Chemical: Imidacloprid.

Prevention: Resistant varieties.

Caterpillars

Damage: Eat leaves.

Organic: Neem spray.

Chemical: Bacillus thuringiensis.

Prevention: Monitor crops.

Flea Beetles

Damage: Small holes in leaves.

Organic: Neem spray.

Chemical: Carbaryl.

Prevention: Row covers.

Corn Earworms

Damage: Damage maize fruits.

Organic: Neem spray.

Chemical: Spinosad.

Prevention: Crop rotation.

Root Maggots

Damage: Attack plant roots.

Organic: Beneficial nematodes.

Chemical: Diazinon.

Prevention: Rotate crops.
