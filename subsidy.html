🌱 Smart Crop Doctor

Market & Schemes

Access government subsidies and financial support for farmers.

Grant

PM-Kisan Samman Nidhi

Annual financial support of ₹6000 to all landholding farmers.

Eligibility

Small and marginal farmers with land ownership.

Insurance

Crop Insurance (PMFBY)

Insurance coverage for crop loss due to natural calamities.

Eligibility

All farmers growing notified crops in notified areas.

Loan

Agriculture Loan Subsidy

Interest subvention on short-term crop loans.

Eligibility

Farmers with active Kisan Credit Card (KCC).
