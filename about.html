🌱 Smart Crop Doctor

AI Powered Smart Farming Assistant

About The Project

Smart Crop Doctor is an AI-based agriculture support system designed to help farmers identify crop diseases quickly and accurately. Using advanced machine learning and image recognition technology, farmers can upload a photo of a diseased leaf and receive instant diagnosis along with treatment recommendations.

The platform also provides real-time weather updates, market price information, crop recommendations and pest control suggestions to help farmers make better agricultural decisions.

🤖 AI Disease Detection

Detect plant diseases instantly by uploading leaf images using deep learning technology.

🌦 Weather Monitoring

Real-time weather forecast helps farmers plan irrigation and crop protection.

📈 Market Prices

Farmers can check mandi prices to sell crops at the best market value.

🌾 Crop Recommendation

AI suggests the best crops based on soil type, temperature and rainfall.

🐛 Pest Control

Identify pests and get recommended solutions to protect crops.

🏛 Government Schemes

Access government subsidies and farmer support schemes easily.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower farmers with modern technology so they can protect crops, increase productivity and improve their income. Smart Crop Doctor aims to bridge the gap between agriculture and technology by providing simple AI tools that any farmer can use easily.
